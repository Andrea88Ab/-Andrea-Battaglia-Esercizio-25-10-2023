Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia apperitze topped whit sweet caramelized onion, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie .it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

featured in:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyound Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 11/2 teasponse instant yeast
  • 11/q teaspons salt
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions thinly sliced
  • 1 tables spoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil forbrushing on top
  • 10 ounces bries cut into cubes ( scrape rind off whit a paring knife if desired, itns not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

Make the focaccia dough

In a medium sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer whit a paddle attachment, mix together all
the dough ingredient until the flour is a fully absorbed .You dont nedd actually knead the
lastof the flour..
